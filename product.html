Our Products Coffee & Espresso

Our espresso bar is equipped with a top-of-the-line Vittoria Arduino Black Eagle, an Italian-made espresso machine, renowned for its precision and ability to extract the perfect espresso shot. We take pride in crafting each coffee beverage with expertise and care, ensuring a smooth, rich, and well-balanced flavor profile in every cup. For those who appreciate specialty brewing methods, our Slow Bar offers a unique experience. We feature a Yama 24 cold brew setup, syphon pots, Hario immersion drippers, and custom V60 pour-over station, designed to highlight the distinct characteristics of each coffee bean variety in different roast levels.

Our Products Salads, Sandwiches & Pastries

At Pacific Bay Coffee Co., we believe in offering healthy and elegant meal options that perfectly complement our handcrafted beverages. Our Salads & Sandwiches selection features fresh, high-quality ingredients, prepared with care to deliver both flavor and nourishment in every bite.

Breakfast & Bowls
Enjoy our delicious breakfast sandwiches and bowls, made fresh to order:

  • Breakfast Sandwich Choice of smoked bacon, sliced ham, or turkey breast served with egg and cheddar cheese on an English muffin, croissant, or bagel.
  • Smoked Salmon Sandwich Fresh smoked salmon with cream cheese, red onion, cucumber, tomato, capers on a bagel.
    Add avocado
  • Classic Avocado Toast Two slices of toasted sourdough bread with avocado, tomatoes, red onion, and feta cheese.
    Add egg
  • Egg Salad & Croissant Egg salad (cucumber, red onion, mustard, mayo) and lettuce on a croissant.
  • Grilled Cheese Sandwich Cheddar cheese on crispy panini sourdough bread with potato chips.
  • Granola Bowl Served with fresh mixed berries, raisins, and your choice of yogurt or milk.

Salads
Our salads are made with crisp greens, vibrant vegetables, and thoughtfully chosen toppings, finished with house-made dressings for a balanced and satisfying meal:

  • Italian Salad – Salami, mozzarella, red onion, olives, cucumber, tomato, pepperoncini, mixed greens, with vinegar dressing
  • Greek Salad – Mixed greens, cucumber, pepperoncini, olive, red onion, feta cheese, with vinegar dressing (add chicken for extra protein)
  • Chicken Salad – Mixed greens, avocado, cucumber, tomato, red onion, olives, and egg with balsamic dressing
  • Smoked Salmon Salad – Organic mixed greens, avocado, tomato, cucumber, olives, red onion, with vinegar dressing

Sandwiches
Our sandwiches are crafted with artisan breads, fresh produce, and high-quality proteins, served with a side of potato chips or spring salad:

  • Classic Turkey Club – Turkey, smoked bacon, tomato, lettuce, pickle, and mayo on toasted sourdough bread
  • Tuna Melt – Tuna salad with cheddar cheese and pickle on panini sourdough bread
  • Chicken Salad Sandwich – Chicken salad (walnuts, green apple, celery, red onion, mustard, and mayo) with lettuce on a croissant
  • Vegetable Sandwich – Spinach, tomato, mozzarella, cheese, and pesto on a panini rosemary focaccia bread
